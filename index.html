Privacy Policy

Universal Session Manager · Last updated July 10, 2026

Universal Session Manager is a browser extension that helps you save, organize, search, and restore your open browser tabs as named "sessions." This page explains what data the extension handles and how it is used.

Local-first by default

All session data — saved tabs, folder structure, tags, notes, favorites, and settings — is stored entirely on your own device, using your browser's built-in local storage (IndexedDB and chrome.storage.local). This data is never transmitted anywhere by default. It never passes through our servers or any third party unless you explicitly enable the optional sync feature described below.

What data the extension accesses

To do its job, the extension reads the following information about your open tabs:

  • Tab URL
  • Tab title
  • Tab favicon (site icon)
  • Whether a tab is pinned
  • Which browser window a tab belongs to

This information is only used to build and restore your saved sessions, and to detect duplicate tabs so you can close them. It is not analyzed, profiled, or used for advertising.

Optional Sync: If you choose to enable the sync feature and connect it to a backend server (either the one provided with this project or your own), your session data — including tab titles and URLs — will be sent to that server so it can be accessed across your devices. This feature is off by default and only activates if you create an account and turn it on yourself in Settings. You can disconnect and disable it at any time, and your data continues to work locally without it.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with any third party.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or tracking.
  • We do not collect analytics on your browsing activity.

Permissions

The extension requests the following browser permissions, each used strictly for the purposes below:

  • tabs — read tab info when saving a session; create tabs when restoring one
  • storage — save your local settings and preferences
  • unlimitedStorage — avoid storage limits as you accumulate saved sessions
  • alarms — schedule auto-save and daily backup snapshots
  • notifications — show a confirmation when a session is saved
  • sessions — let you restore Chrome's recently-closed tabs from the dashboard
  • contextMenus — add a right-click "Save all tabs as a session" shortcut
  • host permissions (<all_urls>) — read tab URLs/titles across any site you have open; no page content is read or modified

Data deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by removing the extension, or by clearing individual sessions from the Trash view in the dashboard. If you have enabled sync, you can request deletion of your synced data by contacting us at the email below.
